Stowe House sits within one of the most important landscape gardens in Europe. Designed by Capability Brown, the grounds contain over forty ornamental temples, monuments, and follies spread across 250 acres of sculpted parkland. The Palladian Bridge, the Temple of Ancient Virtue, and the Grecian Valley provide a sequence of architectural set pieces that no other English estate can match in variety or scale. Inside, the Marble Saloon and the State Rooms offer palatial interiors with original 18th-century decoration. Available exclusively through Luxury London Weddings — a venue that rewards an entire day of exploration with the camera.
