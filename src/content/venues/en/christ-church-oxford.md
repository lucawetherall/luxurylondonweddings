Christ Church is Oxford at its most magnificent. The Great Hall — the inspiration for Hogwarts in the Harry Potter films — features a hammerbeam roof, long oak tables, and walls lined with portraits of former students including thirteen British Prime Ministers. The cathedral itself contains exceptional fan vaulting and the oldest stained glass in England, a fragment dating from 1320. Christ Church Meadow provides a pastoral counterpoint for outdoor shots, with the college's dreaming spires rising above ancient elms. Combining the interior grandeur with the meadow walk creates a complete narrative arc for any pre-wedding portfolio.
